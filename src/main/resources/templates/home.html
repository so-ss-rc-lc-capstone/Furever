Frequently Asked Questions.

Can I Use This App For Any Type Of Pet?

Yes! The app is designed for dogs and their owners to meet up but other pets are welcome to join such as cats, birds, and even more exotic pets.

Is The Website Free To Use?

Yes, as for now the app is completely free to download and use. There are no hidden fees or subscription costs but things may change if more and more pet owners choose to join.

Is My Personal Information Secure On The App?

Yes, the app takes user privacy and security seriously. Your personal information will be kept confidential and secure..

Can I Find Local Pet-Related Events Or Meetups Through The App?

Yes, the app was originally built to allow you to find local pet events and meetups in your area. You can also create and host your own events.

How Can I Create A Profile For My Pet On Furever?

To create a profile for your pet on Furever, simply sign up for an account and go to your profile to add your pet's information and a photo. You can also add additional details about your pet's breed, age, and personality to help connect with other pet owners.

Can I Use Furever To Post Services Such As Adoptions In My Area?

While Furever is primarily a social network for pet owners, you can also use it to search for potential adopters for your pet. Just be sure your pet goes to a loving and responsible new home.

Can I Message Other Pet Owners On Furever?

Yes, Furever has a built-in messaging system that allows you to communicate with other pet owners privately. You can send text messages, photos, and even videos.

Is Furever Only Available In Certain Countries?

No, Furever is available globally! As long as you have access to the internet, you can use Furever to connect with pet owners from all over the world.
